Horror Incident: Allocation Task Force Log

Horror Incident

The incident took place on Planet Thonta, a sizable planet. The Allocation Task Force, known as Prima Pars, has deployed to investigate.
Signal: Strong
Clen: *psss* This is Captain Clen, leader of the Prima Pars. We have successfully landed on Planet Thonta. To my left is an ancient forest, shrouded in mystery, and to my right, an expansive desert. I'm unsure how this planet operates, but for now, everything appears normal... at least for the moment. Over. *pshhh*
---
G Foundation calls back...
*pshhhh*
G Foundation: *pssshh* Hello? One, two, three—if you hear me, please respond.
Clen: *pssshshh* Hello, I hear you.
G Foundation: What’s your signal status?
Clen: Signal is strong.
G Foundation: Okay, we have some questions for you.
Clen: Sure, go ahead.
G Foundation: Did you observe any lifeforms upon landing with the Prima Pars subforce?
Clen: No, nothing out of the ordinary. The only living beings I see are myself and my team members.
G Foundation: That's odd. Our scans indicated that lifeforms are present on this planet.
Clen: Perhaps they are hidden in that forest or the desert? I can’t say for sure.
G Foundation: What’s your plan moving forward?
Clen: My plan is to first explore the forest and then the desert.
G Foundation: Alright then, good luck. Over. *psshhh*
Clen: Over. *pshh*
The call ends, but a day later, Clen initiates contact again...
Clen: *pssshh* Hello, hello! One, two, three—if you hear me, please respond.
G Foundation: *pssshh* Hello, we hear you.
Clen: I think we’ve encountered something unusual... Prima Pars and I traversed the forest and found NO signs of life—no animals, no birds, not even insects...
G Foundation: That’s quite strange. Continue your investigation. If you need anything, just reach out.
Clen: Yes, I will. It’s just... I have a feeling something bad is about to happen.
G Foundation: Don't dwell on those thoughts. If nothing occurs, we’ll sign off.
Clen: Sure... Over. *pshh*
G Foundation: Over. *pshh*
Days turn into silence as 3, 4, 5 days pass without a word from Clen. Something feels wrong.
G Foundation: *psshhh* Hello, hello! One, two, three—if you hear me, please respond.
...
G Foundation: Hello? Is anyone there?
...
G Foundation: Clen? Captain Clen? Are you there?
...
???: *psshhh* You won’t see him again.
G Foundation: Who are you?
???: You won’t see him again.
G Foundation: What do you mean? Where is Captain Clen and the Prima Pars subforce?
???: You won’t see him again.
G Foundation: WAIT WH----- *psshh*
---
Signal was lost for eight months...
In the aftermath, G Foundation has officially declared Clen and the Prima Pars subforce as missing, presumed dead.
