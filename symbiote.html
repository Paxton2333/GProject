Symbiote Invading

Symbiote Incident Log

*This Log was written by: Subforce of A.T.F. (Allocation Task Force), codename Shrine*
*Saeed, the Captain of Subforce, Shrine.*
------
This was written following an incident that happened a few weeks ago. We received a mission that a white goo-like symbiote had landed on a small-sized planet. We, Shrine, went to investigate, but all we found was the meteor, no white symbiote.
We decided to search around the planet; given its small size, we assumed the search would be quick. We used our two spaceships to cover more ground. The only things we encountered were some trees, birds, animals resembling a mix of horses and cows, and some dog-like creatures, but still no sign of the white symbiote.
After weeks of searching without results, we decided to call off the search. As a symbiote, it could have transferred to any living being. We needed to employ our Symbiote Detection System.
Initially, I thought it would be easy. We used a machine designed to gather all living beings on the planet. However, when we activated the Detection System, it yielded no results. Frustrated, I prepared to contact G Foundation to request our retrieval.
Just then, something unexpected happened... I took the Detector and waved it around. To my shock, the light illuminated one of my team members. Although it seemed ineffective, this time, something unimaginable occurred. That member began to scream as their bones and flesh melted, transforming into a white humanoid figure. THIS WAS THE SYMBIOTE WE HAD BEEN SEARCHING FOR, AND IT HAD ENTERED ONE OF MY MEMBER'S BODIES—IT HAD CONSUMED HIM!!!
I activated the alarm, but the symbiote was too quick. It darted around our spaceship, mercilessly attacking my crew. In a desperate move, one of my team members managed to use a Symbiote Storer to contain it. While we succeeded in capturing it, we lost five members—now just piles of melted flesh and bones... God, why did I not notice this earlier...
My Dear God, why did I not notice this member's strange feeling earlier...
