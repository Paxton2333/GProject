103.6 FM Anomaly Incident

30XX/XX/XX
It was a typical morning on Planet Casute, where both children and elders cherished listening to radio stations over television. However, this morning was different...
**06:00 AM, 103.6 FM**
---
*103.6 FM plays*
"Hello, ladies and gentlemen, good morning! This is 103.6 FM! We wish you a wonderful morning and a great day ahead!"
"Today's FM schedule includes:
06:30 - 07:00: Music Station Time
07:00 - 08:00: Morning News
08:00 - 10:00: Stories and Myths
10:00 - 11:00: Food Mysteries
11:00 - 13:00: Noon Time Stories
13:00 - 15:00: Afternoon News
15:00 - 17:00: Yay! Children’s Story and Fun Games!!!
17:00 - 19:00: Dinner Diners
19:00 - 24:00: Nighttime Music"
"So, have a great day!!"
*At 6:30 AM, 103.6 FM begins playing music until 7:00 AM, when morning news is supposed to air. However, something strange occurs...*
*The radio starts to glitch at 7:00 AM*
"psssshh"
"Morning News, today let’s look at-----"
"pssss"
"pssshhhhh"
"..."
"Quisquis hoc audit 103.6 FM, te sentire possum, scio ubi sis, nunc securus non es, celare non potes, scio ubi sis, scio ubi sis, scio ubi sis."
"..."
*Initially, people assumed it was a prank or a hijacking. The transmission returned to normal by 7:30 AM.*
*However, something went wrong at 3:00 PM, during what was supposed to be the Children’s Story fun game time...*
"Hello Children! It’s 3:00 PM!! Let’s do something fun together------"
"psshhh"
"pfsssss..."
"..."
"*glitching sound*"
"..."
"Filii, fac quod dico, exi a domo tua, exi domo tua procul, veni ad silvam, et absconde, et exspectans utere te ad pascendas silvas, exi exi exi exi exi ---- ----"
"*Horror screams*"
*In that moment, the people realized the truth—103.6 FM had malfunctioned, broadcasting something sinister. Many parents quickly turned off the radio and noticed their children behaving strangely, leading to panic and tears.*
*This incident has since become a chilling myth. The G Foundation's A.T.F. dispatched another subforce, named Hydra, to investigate. They translated the ominous messages and found the following meanings:
1. "Quisquis hoc audit 103.6 FM, te sentire possum, scio ubi sis, nunc securus non es, celare non potes, scio ubi sis, scio ubi sis, scio ubi sis" translates to: "Whoever is listening to this 103.6 FM, I can sense you, I know where you are, you are not safe now, you cannot hide, I know where you are, I know where you are, I know where you are."
2. "Filii, fac quod dico, exi a domo tua, exi longe domum tuam, veni ad silvam, et absconde, et exspectans pascere silvas, exi exi exi exi exi" translates to: "Children, do what I say, go outside from your home, leave your home far away, come to the forest, and hide, and waiting to feed the woods, get out get out get out get out get..."*
*Despite extensive searches, no traces were found, and the incident remains an unsettling myth to this day.*
