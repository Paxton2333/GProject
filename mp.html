The Mutation of a Planet

Mutation Planet Incident Log

Date: 30XX/XX/XX
*Log by: Subforce of A.T.F. (Allocation Task Force), codename Cascade*
*Captain Calky, Subforce Cascade*
------
Cascade investigated a peculiar planet—remarkably, this planet itself appears to be alive, with the capacity to move autonomously and a conscious awareness. It has been observed consuming nearby meteors.
We discovered that some meteors it hadn’t yet devoured were showing signs of assimilation, developing flesh-like tissue around them, likely from the planet itself. The planet’s surface is covered in a reddish fluid and dark, brownish-black tissue, which seems to function as its "muscle" or "flesh."
Further investigation revealed that this planet wasn’t always like this. Previously, it was a typical, non-living planet with its own ecosystems and lifeforms. We hypothesize that it underwent a mutation or transformation, possibly after coming into contact with another sentient planet—or perhaps it was invaded by some kind of parasitic planetary organism.
The exact cause remains unknown…
